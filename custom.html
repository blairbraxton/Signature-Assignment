Custom Ordering

Please use this page as a simple guide to place a custom request. After you review the options, reach out on the Contact page to confirm details and timing.

Popular Choices

  • Cake sizes: 6", 8", 10"
  • Frosting: buttercream or whipped
  • Flavors: chocolate, vanilla, red velvet

Lead Time

Most custom orders need 2–3 days. Larger designs may need a little more time.

Notes

Simple, readable decorations are best. If you have a reference photo, attach it when you contact us.
