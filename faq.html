FAQ

Do you bake daily?

Yes, most items are baked each morning while supplies last.

Do you take same-day custom orders?

Usually no because designs need time; but you can ask and we will see.

Do you have gluten-free options?

Limited options may be available; please ask in advance.

What are your hours?

We open most mornings and close when items sell out. Hours may vary on weekends and holidays.

Do you accept prepayment?

Yes, we can take prepayment for custom orders after we confirm the details by email or phone.

Can I make changes to my order?

Small changes are usually fine if requested at least 24 hours in advance. Larger design changes may need more time.
