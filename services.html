Bakery Services

Blair's Bakery offers extra help for guests who need more than a single visit to the counter. We can support event planning, deliveries, and different dietary needs when requested in advance.

  1. Catering and event trays

    We can prepare pastry and bread trays for small gatherings, work meetings, or quiet celebrations.

    • Weekdays: pickup or delivery by request between 8:00 a.m. and 2:00 p.m.
    • Saturday: morning pickups between 8:00 a.m. and 11:00 a.m.
  2. Local delivery

    Pre ordered items can be delivered within nearby Cincinnati neighborhoods when scheduled ahead of time.

    • Available most Tuesdays through Fridays.
    • Typical delivery window: 10:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m.
  3. Support for dietary needs

    We offer guidance for guests who need nut sensitive, dairy light, or egg free choices when our recipes can be adjusted.

    • Questions by email or phone during regular business hours.
    • In person questions welcome in the morning when staff are less busy.
  4. Setup help

    Basic setup help is available so trays and boxes are ready to place on a table when you arrive.

    • Available during pickup times listed above.
    • Please mention setup needs at least one day in advance.

For any of these services, please start with the Custom Ordering page or use the Contact form so we can discuss timing, pricing, and availability.

Nearby Options in Cincinnati

We know that visitors sometimes need more than baked goods alone. Nearby options in the Cincinnati area can add coffee, full meals, and easier access to your visit.

  1. Coffee and tea spots

    There are local coffee shops within a short drive where you can pick up additional drinks or stay for a quiet break.

    • Many open on weekday mornings between 7:00 a.m. and 8:00 a.m.
    • Most close in the late afternoon or early evening.
  2. Casual dining

    Central Cincinnati has casual restaurants that work well for simple celebrations or larger group meals.

    • Typical lunch hours: 11:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.
    • Typical dinner hours: 5:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m.
  3. Parking options

    Public parking garages and surface lots around downtown and Over the Rhine help make arrival more manageable.

    • Garage access is often available from early morning until late evening.
    • Rates and hours vary by location, especially during events.
  4. Transit and streetcar

    Cincinnati Metro bus routes and the streetcar line give guests another way to reach the area without driving.

    • Bus service runs on published schedules throughout the day.
    • The streetcar usually operates daily with frequent stops through downtown and Over the Rhine.

If you would like more specific suggestions that fit your plans, please reach out and we will point you toward nearby options that match the type of visit you have in mind.
